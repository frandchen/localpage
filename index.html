FAQ

How do I import an HTML or ZIP file?

You can import files in three ways:

  • From the Files app, long-press a .html, .htm, or .zip file and choose Share → LocalPage.
  • From any other app (e.g. Mail, Safari, WeChat), use Open with… → LocalPage.
  • Tap the Import button on the Library home screen.
Does LocalPage upload my files?

No. LocalPage works completely offline. All imported files stay on your device and are never sent to any server.

What is the Tip Jar?

The Tip Jar is a fully optional way to support development. It does not unlock any feature or content — every feature in LocalPage is free for everyone. Tips are consumable In-App Purchases and cannot be restored.

How do I export a long screenshot?

Open any document, tap the More menu, then choose Export Long Image. The full page will be captured into a single image you can save to Photos or share via the system share sheet.

The app cannot open my ZIP file.

LocalPage looks for an index.html at the root of the ZIP. If your ZIP doesn't contain one, you'll be shown a file picker to choose the entry HTML manually.
